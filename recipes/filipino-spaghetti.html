Filipino Spaghetti Recipe

Description

Filipino spaghetti is quite different from your regular normal spaghetti. It's tangy, sweet, and has this good taste of home that any Filipino just can't find in any regular spaghetti.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg Spaghetti Pasta
  • Filipino Spaghetti Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Butter
  • Ground Meat (Pork or Beef)
  • Tomato Paste
  • Banana Ketchup
  • Sugar

Directions

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a pan, melt butter and saut√© garlic and onion until fragrant.
  3. Add ground meat and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in Filipino spaghetti sauce. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Add tomato paste to taste.
  6. Add banana ketchup and mix well.
  7. Our secret ingredient is sugar, which balances the acidity of the sauce. Make sure to add it gradually and taste as you go.
  8. Combine the sauce with the cooked spaghetti. Serve with cheese on top.
