Caldereta Recipe

Description

A simple Filipino dish topped up with coconut milk and tomato sauce. A rich and creamy meal for anyone that likes a classic Filipino dish.

Ingredients

  • Meat sliced into cubes (Pork or Beef)
  • Carrots (diced)
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Potatoes
  • Coconut Milk
  • Mama Sita Caldereta
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Tomato Paste
  • Soy sauce

Directions

  1. Heat the pan and then afterwards heat the oil as well
  2. Saute' the garlic and onion
  3. Now put your meat in the pan and let it cook until cooked
  4. Once the meat is drizzle your meat with soy sauce
  5. Now you have to put your potatoes on top of the meat and also putting tomato on top of your potatoes
  6. Once that is done add a bit of water to your Caldereta
  7. Now add your Mama Sita and stir it
  8. The final touch is coconut milk
  9. Once everything is done. Add salt and pepper to taste and then you're done!
